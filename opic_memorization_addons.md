# OPIc 스크립트 암기 보조 데이터

> 각 스크립트에 추가할 3가지: **키워드 뼈대 / 단계 라벨 / 다르게 말하기(패러프레이즈)**
> (진척도 기능은 제외)
> 앱 적용: 모범답안 위 "보기 모드" 토글(전체·단계 라벨·키워드만) + 주제 표현 아래 "다르게 말하기" 섹션.

# Part 2. 롤플레이

## RP1. 전화 오프닝 (첫인사 + 상황 설명)

**[키워드 뼈대]**
Hi, is this [장소]? → help me out → few quick questions about [티켓/예약] → first time → bear with me

**[단계 라벨]**
- 인사 — Hi, is this [장소]?
- 용건 — I'm hoping you can help me out — a few quick questions about [the tickets/my reservation].
- 양해 — This is actually my first time, so bear with me.

**[다르게 말하기]**
- "도와주세요" → I'm hoping you can help me out / Could you help me with something / I could use some help

---

## RP2. 세부 질문하기 (질문 카드)

> 질문 카드는 그 자체가 키워드 역할이라 별도 키워드·단계 라벨 없음. 대신 카드별 "다르게 묻기" 제공.

**[다르게 말하기 — 같은 질문 변형]**
- 가격 → How much is it? / Could you tell me the price? / What's the cost?
- 예약 → Do I need to book in advance? / Should I make a reservation? / Can I just walk in?
- 시간 → What time do you open? / What are your hours? / When do you close?

---

## RP3. 문제 상황 + 사과

**[키워드 뼈대]**
it's me → hate to do this, something's come up → feeling awful all day → splitting headache, can barely move → no way I can make it tonight → feel terrible about bailing

**[단계 라벨]**
- 인사 — Hey [친구], it's me.
- 문제 — I hate to do this, but something's come up — I've been feeling awful, a splitting headache.
- 사과 — There's no way I can make it tonight; I feel terrible about bailing on you.

**[다르게 말하기]**
- "못 갈 것 같아" → I can't make it / I won't be able to make it / I have to bail
- "정말 미안해" → I feel terrible about it / I'm really sorry to do this / I hate letting you down
